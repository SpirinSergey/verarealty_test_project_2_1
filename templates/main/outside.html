BIRD'S - EYE VIEW

Сarlos Ott's bold architectural solution in the construction of the Waldorf Astoria Miami - to present the building as a pile of glass cubes stacked unevenly on the top of each other - creates another unforgettable image of the Waldorf Astoria collection around the world.

It is a breathtaking felling to watch from a height of 1,049 feet above the beauties of Biscayne Bay, the Port of Miami, Brickell Avenue, Downtown Miami, Key Biscayne, and South Beach. An important fact is that the ambitious Waldorf Astoria Miami is the tallest skyskyscraper in Florida.
